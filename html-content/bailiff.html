The Bailiff’s House: Occupied by the Bailiff

(Permanent Power)

  • When a building is freed due to the death of its occupant, the Bailiff can choose a Vagabond of his choice. This Vagabond receives the empty building, hands in their bindle token, and gets the “power token” associated to the building if it had already been used.
  • If, come morning, multiple buildings are free, this selection will take place for each building.

Note: The elimination of the Bailiff will cause the end of the housing distribution. The Bailiff’s house will remain empty. The Vagabonds have thus a vested interest in protecting the Bailiff if they hope to claim a house!
