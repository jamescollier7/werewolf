The Hunter

If he gets devoured by the Werewolves or unfortunately eliminated by the Villagers, the Hunter must strike back before giving up the ghost, by immediately eliminating any player of their choice.

Special case: If the Hunter is in Love with an eliminated player, before leaving the game the Hunter must immediately eliminate another player of their choice. This can create a game where there are no players left alive. In that case, none of the sides can claim victory.
