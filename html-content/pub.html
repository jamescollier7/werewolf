The Pub: Occupied by the Barkeep

(Permanent & Conditional Power)

  • The Werewolves can’t devour him, and the villagers can’t vote against him.
  • Tradition demands that he always votes before anyone else (unless he’s lost his immunity).
  • If he votes against someone who is then eliminated (even if they’re saved before actually being eliminated), he loses his immunity and his token.

Note: All special powers work against him (his immunity is quite relative).
