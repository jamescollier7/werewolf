The Little Girl

The Little Girl can, by opening her eyes a bit, spy on the Werewolves while they’re awake. If she’s caught by one of the Werewolves, she can immediately be devoured (in silence) instead of the chosen victim.

The little Girl can only spy at night, when the Werewolves are awake. She’s not allowed to pass herself off as a Werewolf and open her eyes wide.
