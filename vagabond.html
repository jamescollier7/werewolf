The Vagabonds: No buildings

(Permanent Power)

  • The powers of the Schoolmarm, the Barber, the Scandalmonger, and the Pyromaniac have no effect on the Vagabonds.
  • When a building is freed due to the death of its occupant, the Bailiff chooses a Vagabond of their choice. The former Vagabond receives the empty building, hands in their bindle token, and gets the power token associated with that building.
  • A Vagabond can refuse to occupy a house offered by the Bailiff.
